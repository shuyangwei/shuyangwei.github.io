My name is Shuyang Wei, pronounced as [ʂu iɑŋ, uei]. Welcome to my homepage!

I am a third-year MA student majoring in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics at Zhejiang University (China). Before coming to ZJU, I got a BA at Nanjing University (China) in 2022, where I majored in Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages (TCSOL). From September 2023 to January 2024, I was an exchange student at the University of Manchester (UK), where I got some training in semantics, pragmatics, and learnt some basic knowledge of neurolinguistics and quantitative methods in language science.

My research interests include semantics, syntax and its interaction with semantics, pragmatics, experimental approaches to semantics and pragmatics(although I have not launched any experiments till now.), philosophy of language, typology, neurolinguistics, psycholinguistics, sociolinguistics, and computational linguistics!
