My name is Shuyang Wei, pronounced as [ʂu iɑŋ, uei]. Welcome to my homepage!

I am a third-year MA student majoring in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics at Zhejiang University (China). Before coming to ZJU, I got a BA at Nanjing University (China) in 2022, where I majored in Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages (TCSOL).

From September 2023 to January 2024, I was an exchange student at the University of Manchester (UK), where I got more training in semantics and pragmatics. I also learned some basic knowledge of neurolinguistics and quantitative methods in language science.

My research interests include semantics,syntax-semantics interface, pragmatics, experimental approaches, philosophy of language, neurolinguistics, psycholinguistics, language acquisition, computational linguistics, and typology!

I firmly believe that linguistics is a part of the sciences, not merely the humanities. As a result, I hope I can have some opportunities to work on experimental methods during my PhD.

Feel free to contact me at shuyang.wei@outlook.com!
