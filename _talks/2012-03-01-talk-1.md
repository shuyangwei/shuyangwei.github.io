We utilize the data analysis software CiteSpace to conduct a visual analysis of papers related to Chinese character teaching in the field of Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages (TCSOL), as collected in the China National Knowledge Infrastructure (CNKI) database, by examining three aspects: academic research trends, core research institutions and authors, and keyword clustering. Additionally, it predicts the future research trends in the field in China from five perspectives: new media, Chinese classic culture, new teaching strategies, new textbooks for character teaching, and new missions.
