About

I am from Yongjia, a county located in the southeast of China. Before being a member of Spartans, I received an MA in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics at Zhejiang University, and a BA in Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages at Nanjing University.

Besides linguistics, I also enjoy painting, playing the piano (my piano instructor is Hajin Kim), cooking, anaerobic exercise, reading, writing, collecting quarters, knitting, calligraphy, hiking, and birding.

Ongoing Projects

Prosodic inference in Mandarin wh-in-situ. How do listeners distinguish wh-questions from wh-declaratives when segmental strings are identical? I test how different prosodic cues are weighted and where inference breaks down.
