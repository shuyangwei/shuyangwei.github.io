Hi! My name is Shuyang Wei, pronounced as [ʂu iɑŋ, uei]. Welcome to my homepage!

I'm a first-year PhD student in Linguistics at Michigan State University💚. Before being a member of Spartans, I received an MA in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics at Zhejiang University and had a BA in Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages at Nanjing University.

My research interests include semantics, pragmatics, syntax-semantics interface, experimental methods, and computational modeling.
