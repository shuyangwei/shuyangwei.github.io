About me

I am a first-year PhD student in Linguistics at Michigan State University. My research interests include semantics, pragmatics, syntax-semantics interface, experimental methods, and computational modeling.

Before MSU, I received an MA in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics at Zhejiang University, and a BA in Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages at Nanjing University.
